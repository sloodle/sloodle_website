Makeup

From SLIS Second Life Wiki

Although there's the risk of my avatar looking like Divine from a John Waters movie, makeup is a topic of some interest to a healthy contingent of SL users...and who says you have be in SL as the gender assigned by the random intersection of DNA strands, eh?

So, as you can see:

Makeup includes eyeliner, blush, lipstick, nail polish, etc.

You can change any or all of them according to the various color schemes. Also note that the skin color settings (which are separate but also highly adjustable) have a great deal to do with the aesthetics of your choices, which is very much like real life! re: Divine...and that wonderful pancake base he wore in Pink Flamingos. You can be any color you want. My choice of skin color reflects a desire to have more tan than my good sense will let me acquire in real life...eat your heart out George Hamilton!
