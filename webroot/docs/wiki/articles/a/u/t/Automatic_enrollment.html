Automatic Enrollment

When Automatic Enrollment is turned on, students in Second Life do not have to visit your Moodle webpage in order to start using your Sloodle Tools. Instead, as soon as they sit on a quiz chair, a moodle account will automatically be created for them. The only down side of this, is that if they already have a Moodle account on your Moodle site, their avatar will NOT be linked to it, or if they are a new users, their email address will not be captured. Therefore, it might be preferable to have your users link their Avatar accounts without auto-enrollment turned on. Another side effect of this, is if they choose to log into your webpage later, they will need to first retrieve their auto-generated password. They can do this by clicking on the [Sloodle Password Reset tool].
