Find the SJSU SLIS campus

See below for instructions.


From inside Second Life

  • Click Map on the bottom right and enter "SJSU SLIS" next to the Search button
  • You will see the SJSU logo on the bottom left of the map, click the Teleport button

From a Web Link

(You must have Second Life loaded and have an account)

  • Click on this "Slurl" to see the web index page: http://slurl.com/secondlife/SJSU%20SLIS/128/128
  • Click the Teleport button.

In Second Life, a location is the island name + X/Y coordinates. Our campus is the island "SJSU SLIS" and the center of that 256m2 island is 128/128. SJSU SLIS/128/128

When you arrive

  • You will land in the middle of campus surrounded by buildings
  • Notice the rustic-cabin ranch-style student union to your south and the Tower Hall to your east. Faculty offices are behind the tiki lounge to your east.
  • View a video of our campus plan.
