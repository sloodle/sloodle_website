Navigating Help Island

1. You are now in Orientation Island!

2. You will be heading down a pathway, and when you reach the end you can finally enter the world of Second Life.
3. As you go along the path, you will receive note cards about these subjects: changing appearance, chatting, getting a closer look, grabbing & moving things, and flying.
4. When you fly over the water and land on the platform you see ahead, you will have the option to exit Orientation and get into Second Life.

5. Congratulations, you are now in Second Life.
