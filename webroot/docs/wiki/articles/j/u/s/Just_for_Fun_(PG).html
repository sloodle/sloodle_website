Just for Fun (PG)

From SLIS Second Life Wiki

  • Renaissance Island

If you spend any time in SL, you’ll soon discover that there are many places to buy amenities for your avatar. But the Renaissance-style clothing available here lends an historic, romantic air to your in-world exploration, and the Renaissance village itself has been rendered with some very nice detail.



  • Illusion Factory

This site is gorgeous. The textures and the mood are fantastic. Take the tour to learn about what you can do here. Listen to music, watch videos, dance, play games…



  • Arwenna’s Secret Garden

This relaxing location is beautifully done. Take some time here. You can relax in the spa, chat with friends, and explore the cave.
